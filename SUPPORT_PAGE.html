❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enable alarms?
Start your 7-day free trial to unlock smart prayer alarms. Alarms work even in Do Not Disturb mode!
How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to iOS Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → Waqt → Cancel Subscription. You can cancel anytime during your free trial or paid subscription.
Why aren't alarms firing?
Make sure you have an active subscription and have granted notification permissions. Go to Settings → Waqt → Notifications and enable "Allow Notifications".
How do I change my prayer calculation method?
Go to Settings (gear icon) → Calculation Method. Choose from 7 different methods including Muslim World League, ISNA, Umm al-Qura (Makkah), and more.
What does Ramadan Mode do?
Ramadan Mode automatically enables special alarms for Suhoor (before Fajr) and Iftar (at Maghrib) during the blessed month of Ramadan.
How do I restore my purchase on a new device?
On your new device, open Waqt and tap "Restore Purchase" on the subscription screen. Sign in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.
Is my data private?
Yes! All your prayer data, alarms, and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or share your personal information. Read our Privacy Policy
What are End-Time Warnings?
End-Time Warnings remind you before each prayer time expires. Perfect if you snoozed your first alarm! Set custom timing (15-60 minutes before prayer time ends) in Settings.
